## The Internet

### Yeah, the internet

The Internet is a global network of interconnected computers that communicate using standardized protocols. Since its inception in the late 20th century, it has revolutionized the way we share and access information, conduct business, and connect with people around the world. In this article, we will explore the fundamentals of the Internet, including its infrastructure, how data is transmitted, and its profound impact on modern life.
